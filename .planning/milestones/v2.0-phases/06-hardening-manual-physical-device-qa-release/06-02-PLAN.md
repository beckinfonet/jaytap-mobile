Why a separate Wave-1 plan, not folded into Plan 07 (submission):
1. The audit is independent of the version bump (Plan 05) and the QA matrix walk (Plan 06) — it can run in parallel with Plans 03 (release notes) + 04 (backend redeploy).
2. The audit produces a binding YES/NO disposition that Plan 07 reads. If the audit flags a new data-collecting dep, Plan 07 must NOT proceed with inherited privacy answers.
3. The audit captures re-open conditions per descoped item, so future releases can re-validate inheritance against the audit's rationale.

Output: `06-INHERITANCE-AUDIT.md` with sections — (1) `package.json` diff v1.0.4 vs v2.0.0, (2) per-item inheritance dispositions (PrivacyInfo, ASC App Privacy, Play Data Safety, store listing, Maps key, applinks), (3) re-open conditions, (4) Plan 07 disposition signal.
