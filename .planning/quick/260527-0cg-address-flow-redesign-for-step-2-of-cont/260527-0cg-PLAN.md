<objective>
Redesign Step 2 of `<ContextualListingFlow>` to eliminate the chip-vs-address mismatch
("Pervomaysky District" returned by Nominatim doesn't appear in the curated Bishkek chip
row) and the cross-border geocoder misfires ("15 Lev Tolstoy street" auto-resolving to
a Kazakhstan street). Spec is **LOCKED** at
`.planning/debug/address-flow-redesign.md` (commit `c28c95a`).

Purpose: ship a simpler, more reliable address capture flow (city + map pin + address text)
that lets the citySlug bias actually do its job — without re-architecting the geocoder
or adding a suggestion dropdown.

Output: post-merge,
  1. Step 2 has NO district chip row and NO "Other district" modal.
  2. Address input is gated on city selection (disabled + "Select a city first" helper).
  3. Forward-geocode debounce bumps 300ms → 800ms; citySlug bias guaranteed.
  4. HomeScreen district filter + freetext search match `location.address` text on new listings.
  5. Backend POST validator does NOT reject `district: ''` (audit-and-fix; likely NO-OP).
  6. Display layer is UNCHANGED — legacy listings keep working via the existing
     `address || [district, city].join(' · ')` fallback in `PropertyCard.tsx:84-85` et al.
</objective>
